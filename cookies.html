Cookie Policy

A short, honest disclosure of what cookies and similar technologies this website uses. The short answer: none of our own, and none for tracking.

Effective: 2026-01-15 Last updated: 2026-07-02 Version: 1.0

Our approach

algoveil.tech does not set any first-party cookies. We do not use analytics providers, marketing pixels, session replay tools, or cross-site tracking of any kind.

No consent banner Because we do not set tracking cookies or personal-data cookies of our own, a cookie consent banner is not required for the website in its current configuration.

Third-party requests initiated by this website

A small number of resources are loaded from third-party domains to render the site. These providers may receive your IP address, user agent, and standard HTTP request headers as a technical necessity of delivery. They may set their own cookies according to their own privacy policies — outside our control, but disclosed here for transparency.

You can block third-party cookies in your browser settings without breaking any core functionality of this website.

What we would use in the future

If we add optional features (for example, a scheduling widget, live chat, or privacy-friendly analytics like Plausible or Fathom), we will update this policy before the feature is enabled and, where required by law, ask for consent through a banner.

Browser storage

This website does not use localStorage, sessionStorage, or IndexedDB to store personal or session data.

Do Not Track

Because we do not track visitors, we do not need to respond specially to the Do Not Track signal, but we respect it in spirit — no tracking happens whether or not the header is present.
