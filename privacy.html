Who we are

Algo Veil Technologies, LLC ("Algo Veil", "we", "us") is a software engineering and AI/ML architecture consultancy registered in Delaware, United States. This policy describes how we handle personal information collected via algoveil.tech and during client engagements.

For any privacy question, contact privacy@algoveil.tech.

What we collect

We collect the minimum information required to respond to inquiries and deliver engagements.

Website visitors

  • Contact form submissions — name, email address, company (optional), project type, and message content.
  • Direct email correspondence — content of messages sent to any @algoveil.tech address.
  • Server logs — IP address, user agent, referring URL, and request timestamp, retained for 30 days for security and diagnostics.

We do not use third-party analytics, ad networks, session recording, or cross-site tracking. No advertising cookies are set. See our Cookie Policy for full detail.

Client engagements

  • Business contact information for authorized client representatives.
  • Technical access credentials and data required to deliver the engagement, handled under the Master Services Agreement and Data Processing Addendum in force between the parties.

Lawful basis for processing

Where the GDPR or equivalent regulation applies, we process personal data under one or more of:

  • Legitimate interest — responding to inbound inquiries, operating and securing the website.
  • Contract performance — delivering engagements agreed under an MSA/SOW.
  • Consent — where explicitly given (for example, subscribing to updates, if offered).
  • Legal obligation — retention required by applicable tax, corporate, or accounting law.

Data retention

  • Contact form submissions and inquiries: 24 months after last contact, then deleted.
  • Server logs: 30 days.
  • Client engagement records: as required by the MSA/SOW and applicable law, typically seven years for accounting purposes.

Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent where consent is the basis for processing
  • Lodge a complaint with your data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@algoveil.tech. We aim to respond within 30 days.

California residents Under the CCPA/CPRA you have the additional right to know what personal information we collect, to request deletion, to correct inaccurate information, and to opt out of any sale or sharing of personal information. We do not sell or share personal information as defined under those statutes.

Security

We follow reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal information, including encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+), least-privilege access controls, and audited logging on production systems. For client engagements, additional controls are defined in the MSA/DPA in force.

Algo Veil Technologies, LLC carries professional errors & omissions and cyber liability insurance.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by an updated "Effective" date at the top of this page. Changes take effect on posting; continued use of the website constitutes acceptance.
